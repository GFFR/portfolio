Make a service business operate like a product company — using AI as the lever, not the headline.

A multi-site coworking operator with years of platform intent, ops knowledge in people's heads, and tooling that never quite connected.

CASE FILE 02 · GRIDWORK AUG 2023 — DEC 2025 · ZURICH · CPO

Turn a validated service into a structured B2B product banks and insurers can integrate into.

Ambiguity
Gridwork is the B2B product line that grew out of Brixel's transaction model — same domain, new buyer. Enterprise partners wanted APIs and process standards, not the consumer-facing service the team had been optimising for.
Clarity
Framed the roadmap around what financial institutions, agencies, and internal ops each needed to see — then made partner requests compete with product surface area instead of one-off builds.
Execution
Ran product while Brixel and Gridwork overlapped in calendar — protecting continuity for existing users while opening the enterprise lane. Detail in v5.0.
Outcome
Swiss banking and insurance partners on a structured product architecture. Integrations moved from bespoke projects to repeatable product capabilities.
